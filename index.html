About

I am a Project Lecturer (特任講師) in the Graduate School of Economics at the University of Tokyo. I completed my PhD in Economics at the University of Warwick, following postgraduate study at the University of Bath and undergraduate study at the Central University of Finance and Economics (CUFE) in Beijing. I was born in Dongguan, Guangdong, China.

My research applies computational and statistical methods — including reinforcement learning and deep-learning techniques — to problems in macroeconomics and econometrics. I also teach computational economics to graduate students at the University of Tokyo.
